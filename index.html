Happy Tails Pet Photography.

We all love to see our pets happy and content! Here at Happy Tails, we have a dream, to make our photoshoots happy, relaxed and fun for our beloved pets. Our aim is to portray them in their element, as they go about their walks, jumping in puddles, have fun in the park or relax after a long walk or cuddle with you.

We’d like to make our photoshoot memorable, a day to put a smile on your face and lots of wiggly tails!

How it works.What we do.

After booking your photoshoot, we would like to find out as much as possible about your pet. This information will be useful in planning our photoshoot ahead. We would like to know how old your pet is, what size or breed, if he’s food or play motivated. If it’s an active dog or if he/she can sit and wait on command and if they are friendly with other dogs and animals.

We would encourage you to be in the picture with your dog, from our experience, these are some of the shots people cherish the most.

We always listen to you! If you have special requirements or ideas, please let us know!

Hi! My name is Eva, I'm the pet photographer!

Ever since I can remember I loved all animals, big and small. As grew up on a farm when I was little, most of the times I feel more at ease between animals than humans. I grew up taking care of cows, sheep, bunnies, cats and dogs. Some were my best friends.

I took my love my of animals and the passion for photography to become a pet photographer. I’m a professional photographer with a Photography degree from Griffith College.

I’m looking forward to photograph all the lovely doggies and kitties, horses and their human friends!
